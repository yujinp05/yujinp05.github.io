Yujin Park

Assistant Professor

Yujin Park is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Data Science at Yonsei University (MIRAE) , Republic of Korea.

She received her Ph.D. in Statistics from Ewha Womans University, where she was advised by Professor Jae Keun Yoo.

Prior to joining Yonsei University, she worked as an engineer at Samsung Electronics, specializing in statistical quality control and data analysis for manufacturing optimization.

Her research interests include sufficient dimension reduction, functional data analysis, machine learning, and data science.
